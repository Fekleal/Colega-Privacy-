Coléga (“the App”) is provided by Felipe Kleine Albers (“we”, “us”). This policy explains how information is handled when you use the App.

Plain summary: We don’t create accounts or ask for your personal details. The App displays ads using Google AdMob, which may process device identifiers to deliver and measure advertising. The App fetches publicly available cafeteria menus from university websites; we do not collect personal data from those sites.

1) Information We Collect

We don’t directly collect personal information. While using the App, third-party services (notably Google AdMob) may process:

  • Device identifiers (e.g., Advertising ID), IP address, coarse location inferred from IP, and ad interaction signals (impressions/clicks), plus diagnostics.

Those data are handled under the third party’s policies. See Google Advertising – Privacy & Terms and AdMob policy.

2) Ads & Analytics

The App uses Google Mobile Ads (AdMob). You can manage ad personalization in Android (Settings → Google → Ads) and via Google Ad Settings.

3) University Menu Content

The App loads public cafeteria menu pages from official university domains to display current menus. We are not affiliated with the universities and do not claim ownership of their content. We do not combine this content with personal data.

4) Permissions

  • Internet / Network state: to fetch menus and load ads.
  • Notifications (optional): if enabled, used for simple app alerts (no personal data required).

5) Children’s Privacy

The App targets a general audience and is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect children’s personal information.

6) Data Retention

We don’t store personal data on our own servers. Any ad-related data are retained by Google under its policies. Any cached menu data are transient and non-personal.

7) Security

We aim to minimize data collection. Internet services carry inherent risks; keep your device updated.

8) International Transfers & Legal Bases

Ad services may process data on servers outside your country. Where applicable (e.g., LGPD, GDPR), Google acts as an independent controller for advertising. We act as the app publisher and do not receive personal data from AdMob.

9) Your Choices

  • Limit ad personalization in device settings and via Google Ad Settings.
  • Uninstall the App to stop all data flows from the App.
  • Contact us if you believe we hold personal data and want deletion (see Contact).

10) Changes

We may update this policy. We’ll post changes here and update the date above.
